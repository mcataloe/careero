Careero product decisions should preserve these principles:

- Job seeker first. The user owns their search data, strategy, artifacts, and visibility.
- Local first until durable value and production readiness are proven.
- COMPASS is advisory, explainable, and source-grounded; it is not an opaque authority.
- Automation is review-first and auditable. External or state-changing automation requires explicit approval and fresh LEAP review.
- Monetization must not compromise privacy, ranking integrity, or user trust.
- Employer, recruiter, marketplace, and hosted collaboration features remain later-layer work behind strict consent and governance boundaries.

Use [product strategy](00_product-strategy.md) for the full layer plan and current build posture.
